Game Instructions:

  • This game is a memory card game, and the objective is simply to match the cards according to symbols (and colors)
  • There are two rounds in total -
    • Round one: Match the cards by the symbols and colors
    • Round two: Match the cards by ONLY the symbols
  • Fire away and test your memory! Hint: focus on the symbols
